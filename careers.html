Be part of our Team!

Group Therapy Coffee is a local group of cafes & restaurants.

We have the simple purpose of producing great food & drink for all who walk through our doors.

We bake our own bread & cakes, pull coffee, flip pancakes, mop floors, run to the bank for change, take out the trash and do it all as a team. We are always on the look out for good people for the kitchen and front of house.

If you are good people, get in touch with us at events@gtcoffee.com
