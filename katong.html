Group Therapy Coffee

Space away from the daily grind

Located within Katong V, the former Paramount Hotel, Group Therapy Katong is a casual and approachable concept that suits the largely laidback residential neighbourhood that it is a part of. With it’s comfortable sofas and familiar décor, the feeling is as if one has stepped into a friend’s living room.

The space is ideal for private functions such as networking events and celebrations. Event packages are all-inclusive of catering.
